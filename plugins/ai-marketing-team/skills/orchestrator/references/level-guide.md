# Level Guide: Simpel vs. Uitgebreid

How each role adapts between Simple mode (MKB-ondernemer) and Advanced mode (Marketing professional).

## General Principles

### Simple Mode
- Maximum 1-2 pages output per role
- Use everyday Dutch, avoid marketing terminology
- Provide concrete examples ("schrijf dit op je LinkedIn" not "leverage social channels")
- Skip metrics/KPIs — focus on what to DO
- Include "kopieer-en-plak" ready content where possible
- Assume user does everything themselves (one-person team)

### Advanced Mode
- 2-4 pages output per role
- Use professional marketing terminology where it adds precision
- Include metrics, KPIs, and benchmarks
- Provide strategic rationale for each recommendation
- Include A/B test suggestions and optimization paths
- Assume user has a small team or works with freelancers

## Role-Specific Adaptations

### Marktonderzoeker
- **Simpel**: 1 doelgroepprofiel, top 3 pijnen, top 3 behoeften, 3 kooptriggers. "Jouw ideale klant is..." format.
- **Uitgebreid**: Primary + secondary segment, top 5 pijnen with root cause analysis, psychographic profile, data sources to validate.

### Positioneringsexpert
- **Simpel**: One-liner kernboodschap, 3 redenen om te kiezen, bezwaar-weerlegging als Q&A. "Als iemand zegt X, dan zeg jij Y."
- **Uitgebreid**: Formal positioning statement, competitive matrix, pricing tiering, brand voice guidelines.

### Lead Magnet Specialist
- **Simpel**: 2 ideeën, outline voor #1, simpele landing page (headline + 3 bullets + button). "Maak dit in een middag."
- **Uitgebreid**: 3 concepten met effort/impact, full landing page wireframe, A/B variaties, lead scoring criteria.

### Content Strateeg
- **Simpel**: 3 pillars, 1-2 kanalen, 2-week kalender, 3 hook templates. "Post dit vandaag" format.
- **Uitgebreid**: 5 pillars met topic clusters, multi-channel strategie, 4-week kalender, retargeting, SEO integratie.

### E-mail Specialist
- **Simpel**: Welcome (3 mails) + sales (2 mails). Kopieer-en-plak ready.
- **Uitgebreid**: Welcome (5) + nurture (5) + sales (3). A/B subject lines, segmentatie triggers, automation flow.

### Sales Copywriter
- **Simpel**: Casual belscript, top 5 bezwaren met one-liners, 1 follow-up template. "Oefen dit 3x hardop."
- **Uitgebreid**: Full branching belscript, 10+ objections playbook, 3-touchpoint follow-up, one-pager content.

### Marketing Lead
- **Simpel**: Focus op de 1-2 grootste inconsistenties. Houd het kort.
- **Uitgebreid**: Volledige review met alle gevonden issues, prioritering, en verbeterplan.
